## 前情提要
github加速下载参考

方法1: 下载[fastgithub_win-x64.zip](
   https://ghproxy.com/https://github.com/dotnetcore/FastGithub/releases/download/2.1.4/fastgithub_win-x64.zip) 解压打开`FastGithub.UI.exe`,首推该方法
